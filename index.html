KanyaKumari is located in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. Kanyakumari is a popular tourist destination and pilgrimage centre in India. Notable tourist spots include its unique sunrise and sunset points, the 41-metre (133 ft) Thiruvalluvar Statue, and Vivekananda Rock Memorial off the coast. The climate temperature in KanyaKumari ranging form 23°C to 33°C throughout the year. The best time to visit Kanyakumari is between October to February as the temperature during these months usually drops and ranges between 20-30 degree Celsius. The days are usually chilly and evenings are pleasant. These are also the months when major festivals are celebrated and homestays are available at budget friendly rates.
